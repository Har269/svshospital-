Overview

SVS Medical College is situated in Mahbubnagar in Telangana state of India. Established in 1999, it is accredited from DCI, MCI and it is affiliated to NTR University of Health Sciences. SVS, Mahbubnagar offers 7 courses across 5 streams namely Medical, Vocational, Dental, Nursing, Paramedical and across 5 degrees like MBBS, BSc, BDS, MDS, PGD. SVS campus is spread over 52 Acres. Hostel facility is not available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Auditorium, Boys Hostel, Computer Lab, Hospital, Library are also there.
